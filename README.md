# ![Icon](https://github.com/borinvini/MountainShooter/blob/main/asset/Player1.png?raw=true) Mountain Shooter

**Mountain Shooter** is a retro-style 2D arcade side-scroller developed with **Python** and **Pygame**. Experience fast-paced combat, strategic movement, and a challenging progression system in this classic space adventure.

---

## 🎮 Game Features

- **Three Challenging Levels:**
  - **Levels 1 & 2:** Survival waves with increasing difficulty and unique environments.
  - **Level 3 (Final Frontier):** The climax of the game featuring elite enemies and the ultimate Boss.
- **Multiplayer Co-op:** Play solo or with a friend in **2-Player Local Co-op** mode.
- **Advanced AI & Mechanics:**
  - **Elite Enemies (Enemy3):** Sinusoidal (zigzag) movement patterns for unpredictable combat.
  - **Epic Boss Battle:** A massive fortress with $1000\ HP$ that appears as the final challenge.
- **Global Score System:** Integrated **SQLite3** database to save and track high scores.
